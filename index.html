If days run long · overtime structure

Live event days run 12 hours by default; prep and wrap days run 16 hours and already include four hours of overtime built into the day rate. If Fendi's team needs to extend any day beyond its window:

  • · Base hourly rate (within standard window) — $4,219/hr
  • · Overtime rate (each hour beyond the standard window) — $6,330/hr · time-and-a-half on the base

Overtime is billed in 30-minute increments. Cocoon will track and bill at end of run; if extension exceeds 4 hours on any single day, we'll flag for advance approval.

Overnight security · confirmed with venue

Two overnight security personnel will be in the building during closed-hours each night to safeguard the inventory on display (clothing, jewelry, accessories). The venue has confirmed the following:

  • · No additional venue charge for the overnight security presence — the team will be locked in for the closed-hours window at no fee from Academy Mansion.
  • · The security service itself (the personnel) is a pass-through cost we'll source from Cocoon's vetted vendor list at this caliber.
  • · Final figure for the security service follows once we lock the specific vendor — we'll bring it forward before the contract signature window opens.
Credit-card hold · $75,000

Standard for engagements at this scale. The hold sits on a credit card across the duration of the reservation and covers potential overage exposure: overtime extensions, additional cleaning, damage to the building, or garbage overage. Nothing is charged unless one of those occurs. The hold releases in full at the end of the run, less any actual overage incurred. The figure is sized to comfortably cover ~12 hours of OT, the most likely category of overage on a week-long run.

Certificate of Insurance (COI) · required

Standard requirement for venues at this caliber. Fendi will need to provide a COI from its commercial liability insurer ahead of load-in:

  • · Naming Academy Mansion as additional insured, with coverage limits the venue specifies in the contract (typically $1–2M general liability)
  • · Cocoon Flex Spaces also listed as additional insured
  • · Must be in place before load-in on Sept 6
  • · Your insurance broker can typically issue a project-specific COI within 24–48 hours once we share the venue's exact coverage requirements

We'll attach the venue's COI requirements to the contract package so the broker has everything in one place.

Locking the dates · hold-challenge sequence

Casa Mas has existing holds across most of the Sept 6–13 window. Issuing Fendi's hold means challenging them.

When Cocoon formally challenges the existing holds, competing holders have 48 business hours to either confirm or release. Releases are typically immediate. Once holds clear, Fendi has 24 hours to sign the contract and wire the 50% deposit — or the dates open again.

Sequence — Fendi side
  1. Final sign-off on pricing and scope (Scenario 1 vs Scenario 2)
  2. Contract finalized between Cocoon and Fendi's legal team (review currently underway)
  3. 50% deposit pre-staged with Fendi's treasury team — wire-ready
  4. Green light to Cocoon to issue the hold challenge
  5. Within 24 hours of hold release: sign contract + wire deposit
Sequence — Cocoon side
  1. Hold contract + deposit instructions ready in parallel with Fendi's prep
  2. Issue the hold challenge on Emily's confirmation
  3. Track the 48-hour window with existing holders
  4. Notify Emily the moment holds clear (typically immediate)
  5. Receive signed contract + deposit within the 24-hour window

Steps 1 – 3 happen on Fendi's timeline. Step 4 starts only when Emily confirms readiness. We'll coordinate the exact issue date with you so the 48-hour window lines up with when the contract and deposit are both wire-ready.
