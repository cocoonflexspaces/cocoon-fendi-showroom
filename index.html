What the showroom is doing

Hosting Fendi's top clients across a full week — by appointment, in measured numbers. Presenting new collections in a setting where the architecture does the framing and the conversation is unhurried. Floor staff and tailors moving discreetly between rooms, clients arriving and leaving at their own pace.

What the venue needs to deliver

Rooms that feel intentional and quiet — not branded, not staged. Enough square footage to keep separate moments running simultaneously (a presentation room, a tailoring corner, a service space, a back-of-house). Daylight where possible, exclusive access throughout the run, and the kind of address Fendi's clients already know how to find.

Included with every venue figure in this deck

An on-site Cocoon Casa Manager for the full duration of the reservation — concierge presence, vendor coordination, on-the-day problem-solving, the standard Casa Manager service set you'd expect at any Cocoon property. Plus unlimited Super Concierge support for the run — services, sourcing, last-minute requests, anything Fendi's team needs to dispatch quickly without leaving the building.
